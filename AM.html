The Asharah Mubasharah are ten companions of the Prophet ﷺ who are guaranteed jannah by the Prophet ﷺ

Abu Bakr As-Siddeeq رضي الله عنه

Umar ibn al-Khattab رضي الله عنه

Uthman ibn Affan رضي الله عنه

Ali ibn Abi Talib رضي الله عنه

Talha ibn Ubaidullah رضي الله عنه

Zubair ibn al-Awwam رضي الله عنه

Abdur-Rahman ibn Awf رضي الله عنه

Sa’id ibn Zayd رضي الله عنه

Abu Ubaidah ibn al-Jarrah رضي الله عنه

Sa’ad ibn Abi Waqqas رضي الله عنه
