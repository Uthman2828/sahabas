The Sahaba

The Asharah Mubasharah

The term “Sahaba” (singular: Sahabi) refers to the companions of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. They were the men and women who had the honor of meeting the Prophet ﷺ during his lifetime, believed in his message, and followed him in word and deed. They are role models for us as they rarely sinned. The four closest Sahaba to the Prophet ﷺ were:

  • Abu Bakr رَضِيَ ٱللَّٰهُ عَنْهُ
  • Umar ibn Al-Khattab رَضِيَ ٱللَّٰهُ عَنْهُ
  • Uthman ibn Affan رَضِيَ ٱللَّٰهُ عَنْهُ
  • Ali ibn Abi Talib رَضِيَ ٱللَّٰهُ عَنْهُ
